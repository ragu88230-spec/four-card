ICC T20 World Cup

1.Introduction

1.The T20 World Cup is an international cricket tournament organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC).

2.It is played in the Twenty20 (T20) format, where each team plays 20 overs.

2.Conducted

1.The tournament is held once every 2 years.

3.First Tournament

1.The first T20 World Cup was held in 2007 in South Africa.

2.The first winner was India.

4.Format

1.International teams from different countries participate.

2.The tournament includes:

  1. Group Stage
  2. Super 8 Stage
  3. Semi-finals
  4. Final

ICC ODI World Cup

1.Introduction

1.The ODI World Cup is the most prestigious One Day International cricket tournament organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC).

2. Each match is played with 50 overs per team.

2.Conducted

1.The tournament is held once every 4 years.

3.First Tournament

1.The first ODI World Cup was held in 1975 in England.

2.The first winner was West Indies.

4.Format

1.International teams from different countries participate.

2.International teams compete in:

  1. league Stage
  2. KnockOut Stage
  3. Semi-finals
  4. Final

ICC Champion Tropy

1.Introduction

1.The Champions Trophy is an international One Day International (ODI) cricket tournament organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC).

2.Only the top-ranked cricket teams participate in this tournament.

2.Conducted

1.The tournament is held once every 4 years.

3.First Tournament

1.The first Champions Trophy was held in 1998 in Bangladesh.

2.It was originally called the ICC KnockOut Trophy.

4.Format

1.Limited number of top teams participate.

2.Matches include:

  1. Group Stage
  2. Semi-finals
  3. Final

ICC Test Championship

1.Introduction

1.The World Test Championship (WTC) is an international cricket competition organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC).

2.It is played in the Test cricket format, which is the longest form of cricket.

2.Conducted

1.The championship cycle is conducted once every 2 years.

3.First Tournament

1.The first World Test Championship started in 2019.

2.The first final was won by New Zealand in 2021.

4.Format

1.It determines the world champion of Test cricket.

2.It gives global context and competition to bilateral Test series.
